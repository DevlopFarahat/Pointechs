How it works

  • Register your business at (www.Pointechs.com) and setup your branding with your logo and your loyalty program.
  • Configure your branches and add your Cashiers.
  • Your loyalty card is now available in Pointechs App so ask your customers to download it (if they don’t have it already). Let them register and issue their digital loyalty card.
  • Cashiers can scan customer’s digital card, or just use their mobile to reward or redeem points.
  • Measure the effectiveness of your loyalty program and keep track of points/stamps issued and used by each customer.
